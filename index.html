Welcome!

This is a page about my Pokémon team! I love these games a ton, and I have recently become interested in competitive battling. I am part of a discord server that is hosting a tournament this weekend and I thought I would make a very brief page about it! I play the generation 7 games, Sun, Moon, and Ultra Sun and Moon. General info about my team is found below! Follow this link to learn more: Click Here

Landorus!

Landorus is an incredibly defensive utility pokemon. Every time he is sent in, he lowers the opposing pokemon's attack stat. He can set up entry hazards for the other side, and switch out easily with one of his moves, U-Turn. He is crucial to putting pressure on strong pokemon and reducing switches.

Magearna!

Magearna is a pure meat shield! It can always be switched in to sponge any special attack, and deal tons of damage as well.

Tapu Kolo!

Tapu Koko is insanely fast, and it's damage output puts pressure on any pokemon it's up against.

Medicham!

Medicham gets the highest base attack in the game, paired with a move that always goes first and flinches the target, fake out, every time it is sent in, it is always a threat.

Kartana!

Kartana is able to outspeed basically any other pokemon. He is quite frail but hits ridiculously hard.

Rotom!

Rotom is essential, due to its' ability Levitate, which makes it immune to entry hazards and ground moves, which makes it perfect to use the move defog, which clears entry hazards.
